About

wools++ is a community-driven and fan-made project. The part that does stuff is written in python, and it uses jinja2 with bootstrap html/css/javascript templates for displaying. wools++ uses BeautifulSoup to collect data from users' profiles at OC and matplotlib to produce the graphs. The project is hosted on Google's App Engine. All of the source code can be found here.

The project is currently in the experimental stage, so you may encounter some issues or bugs. Sorry about that!


-Ben

FAQ

My stats aren't updating!
wools++ only collects data a couple times per day (once every six hours, to be exact). This is to limit requests made to OC's website, as well as to limit the work that the app has to do. The frequency at which data is collected may change in the future. Remember, wools++is more about "long term" stats than up-to-the-second ones.
My stats aren't accurate / I found an arbitrary problem!
Please report all problems in the official wools++ thread on OC's forum.
What's a rolling stat?
A rolling stat is a stat that is computed with only a given amount of recent data. For example, a 2 day rolling KD would be computed as (kills earned in the last 2 days) / (deaths earned in the last 2 days). As of now, wools++ computes rolling stats based on the last 7 days of playing time. This time period is totally arbitrary, but it seems to be okay. One of the advantages of a rolling stat is that it gives a player feedback much more quickly than a standard statistic that includes everything, such as (all kills)/(all deaths). More info here.
Why don't I have any rolling stats graphs / why don't they show up in my data table?
If you haven't been in the database for the longer than one rolling stat interval (7 days), you won't see any rolling stats because they need at least one full interval to compute them.
What's a "RWD"?
Common abbreviations used:
  • K=Kills
  • D=Deaths
  • C=Cores
  • W=Wools
  • M=Monuments
  • O=Objectives
  • S=Scoreboxes (just kidding!)
  • R=Rolling
Most stats are computed as something/deaths. So, KD would be kills/deaths. "RWD" means "rolling wools deaths" or (wools gotten in the past 7 days)/(deaths in the past 7 days)
How do I put a graph on my profile on OC?
Copy the link in the box under the graph labeled "HTML" and paste it into the "About Me" section of your profile. If you do, it would be very much appreciated if you provided a link to this site or the corresponding forum post. :)
